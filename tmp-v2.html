Vanicream™ Gentle Facial Cleanser

Our mildest cleanser specifically formulated for daily facial use. Helps remove makeup and excess skin oils without drying your skin. Leaves skin feeling clean and soft. Ideal for dry skin. 

Now available in a convenient 2.5 ounce travel size!

Formulated for Sensitive Skin

The Most Important Ingredient Is Trust™

A soap-free formula that is gentle to even the most delicate and sensitive skin. Non-comedogenic (won't clog pores), no harsh cleansing agents, and pH balanced. No botanical extracts, no essential oils. Sulfate-free. Gluten-free. Dermatologist tested. Awarded the National Eczema Association Seal of Acceptance.

Learn More About Chemical Irritants

water, glycerin, coco-glucoside, sodium cocoyl glycinate, acrylates copolymer, caprylyl glycol, mica, sodium chloride, 1,2-hexanediol, titanium dioxide, sodium hydroxide, disodium EDTA

Ingredient Information

Apply generous amount to wet skin, lather, rinse, and pat dry with towel. Ideal for daily use.

  1. Is Vanicream™ Gentle Facial Cleanser drying?
    1. No, it is specifically designed not to dry out the skin. Our Gentle Facial Cleanser cleanses while leaving your skin feeling clean, soft, and comfortable. However, individual results may vary.

  1. How often should I use this cleanser?
    1. Our Gentle Facial Cleanser is mild and gentle enough to use twice daily – perfect for a morning and nightly cleanse.

  1. Is the Vanicream™ Gentle Facial Cleanser suitable for all skin types?
    1. This cleanser is ideal for dry, sensitive, and delicate skin types. The gentle, soap-free formula is designed to cleanse without irritation.

  1. What makes this cleanser “soap-free”?
    1. Our Gentle Facial Cleanser formula contains non-soap surfactants, making our cleanser milder and gentler on the skin barrier.

  1. Is Vanicream™ Gentle Facial Cleanser suitable for all ages?
    1. This cleanser is suitable for all ages and specifically designed for individuals who need or want to avoid common chemical irritants.  You could try our Vanicream™ Foaming Wash for Baby for more delicate skin.

  1. Will this cleanser remove makeup?
    1.  Yes, our Gentle Facial Cleanser removes makeup and excess skin oils. For waterproof makeup, a second cleanse may be needed.

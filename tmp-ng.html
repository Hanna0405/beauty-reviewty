Neutrogena Hydro Boost Hyaluronic Acid Water Gel with signature fragrance instantly delivers deep, weightless hydration for refreshed, quenched, dewy skin. This ultra-lightweight gel instantly refreshes and cools skin, absorbing quickly for deep, long lasting hydration without feeling too heavy. Ideal for normal, oily and combination skin types, this lightweight formula is also suitable for acne-prone skin. Formulated with hyaluronic acid, a dermatologist recommended hydrator that binds to water and holds it within skin's surface. Formula is dermatologist-tested, clinically-proven to deliver 24-hour hydration and strengthens skin's moisture barrier after just one use. This formula is non-greasy, noncomedogenic, and won’t clog pores. Add this water gel moisturizer to your daily skincare routine with other hydrating products from Neutrogena.
